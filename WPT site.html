About WPT

BUILT ON A HIGHER STANDARD

Winners Personal Training was founded after years of observing a clear gap in the Dutch personal training market: most trainers lack structure, clear progression, or the professional development required to consistently deliver results.

WPT combines evidence-based training, nutritional guidance, and lifestyle coaching into one coherent system. The goal is not to create dependency — it's to build capable, independent clients.

Based in Groningen, with an international coaching model designed for the Dutch and Canadian markets.

The Foundation Program

4 MONTHS. ONE SYSTEM.

The Winners Foundation Program is a time-bound, outcome-driven coaching trajectory. You'll train with structure, understand why every method is applied, and graduate with the tools to perform independently.
